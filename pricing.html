Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing

How do credits work?

Each image generation consumes 10 credits. With our Pro plan's 500 credits, you can generate 50 high-quality images per month. Credits reset at the beginning of each billing cycle.

Can I change or cancel my plan anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle, and you keep access to your current plan until then.

Do unused credits roll over?

Currently, credits reset each month and don't roll over. However, we're working on a credit banking system that will allow you to save unused credits for future use.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and various international payment methods. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Every new user starts with 20 free credits to try our service. This lets you generate 2 images and experience the quality before deciding on a paid plan.

Can I use generated images commercially?

Yes! All images generated with Flux Krea AI can be used for commercial purposes without any additional licensing fees. You own the rights to your generated content.
