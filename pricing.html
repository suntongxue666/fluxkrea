Frequently Asked Questions

What is FLUX Krea AI?

FLUX Krea AI is an advanced AI image generation platform that uses cutting-edge artificial intelligence technology to help users easily create high-quality images. Whether you're a designer, artist, or content creator, our tools can help bring your creative ideas to life.

How do I choose the right plan for me?

Choosing a plan depends on your usage needs. If you're an occasional user, the Free plan is enough to get started. For regular creators who need more generations and advanced features, the Pro plan is ideal. Power users and professionals who need maximum quality and volume should consider the Max plan.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. After cancellation, your subscription will continue until the end of the current billing cycle. We don't provide partial refunds, but you can continue using the service until your subscription period ends.

Who owns the copyright to generated images?

Images generated using our platform belong to you. You can use these images for personal or commercial purposes without any restrictions. However, please note that you should comply with relevant laws and regulations and not generate illegal or infringing content.

What payment methods do you support?

We support various payment methods, including credit cards and PayPal. All payments are securely encrypted, and we don't store your complete payment information.
